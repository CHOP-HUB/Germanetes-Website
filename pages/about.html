Germanetes Jardins d'Emma is a non-profit organization located around the boulevard of the neighborhood, the Green Axis of Consell de Cent. We are organized around two main objectives: generating community in the neighborhood and making it more sustainable, collaborating with the environment to achieve an active community that includes residents, schools, other entities, collectives and cooperatives in the neighborhood and public institutions with social objectives.

The association's projects are as follows: Germanetes Space, the community garden; Nur Space, the community garden; Aula Viva, the pedagogical project; Revolta Eixample, community composting project; Vine a Jugar, children's play space, and the Resource Bank, a project for lending materials to neighborhood entities and collectives.

The association seeks to be as democratic, horizontal and transparent as possible. This is why each project has its own governance body, and representatives from all projects meet biweekly as a coordination team and the engine of the association; assemblies and meetings are open to everyone. Finally, each January the general assembly is held, which is the body with the most power within the association.

If you feel like participating in one of the projects, just get in touch with us!
